## The problem we solve

Ops, sales, and support teams drown in repetitive cross-tool work:
triaging inboxes, updating the CRM, chasing leads, filing tickets. Hiring
more people for it is slow and expensive; "AI copilots" still need a
human driving every click.

## What we do

- **AI employees, not copilots.** Each agent has its own identity,
  mailbox, and channel. You delegate outcomes, not prompts.
- **Real integrations.** Native OAuth into 250+ tools (Gmail, Slack,
  HubSpot, Apollo, Stripe, …) — no scrapers, no browser puppets.
- **Policy-gated autonomy.** Per-action rules: auto-send routine email,
  draft refunds, never delete data. You set the leash length.
- **Full audit trail.** Every tool call, decision, and dollar spent is
  replayable.
