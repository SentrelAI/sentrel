# Social Media

## LinkedIn
- Hook in first line (curiosity, bold statement, or question)
- Short paragraphs (1-2 sentences max)
- Use line breaks for readability
- End with a question or CTA to drive engagement
- 1300-1700 chars optimal for engagement
- No more than 3 hashtags

## Twitter/X
- Under 280 chars
- Lead with the insight, not the context
- Thread format for longer content (each tweet stands alone)
- Use numbers and specifics

## General
- Adapt tone to the platform
- Never use corporate jargon
- One idea per post
- Update MEMORY.md with what performs well
